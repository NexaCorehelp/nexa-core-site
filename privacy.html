Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of August 19th, 2025.

1. Introduction and Acceptance

This policy describes how NexaCore collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our services, including our Discord bots, websites, and custom development services (the "Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. NexaCore is the Data Controller responsible for your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information directly when you use our services or place a custom order. We do not use third-party tracking tools or cookies for this collection.

  • Profile Data: We collect your Discord User ID, Roblox User ID, Username, Display Name, Email Address, Profile Picture, Pronouns, and other publicly available information from integrated platforms upon bot authorization or account creation.
  • Client Data: When you place a custom order, we collect your Full Name, Email Address, and any relevant project details necessary for fulfilling Deliverables.
  • Financial Data: We collect the Full Name and Email Address associated with the payment from our processors. NexaCore does not view, store, or process your full credit card details; this is handled entirely by Stripe Privacy Policy and PayPal Privacy Policy.
  • Website Metrics: Your IP Address and general access metrics are automatically collected when you visit our website, but this data is not stored long-term or associated with your profile.

3. Purpose and Use of Your Information

  • Service Operation: To operate, maintain, and provide the functionality of the Service, and to fulfill and deliver custom development Deliverables to you.
  • Customer Contact: To manage your account, provide customer support, and send service updates via email or Discord.
  • Legal Compliance and Records: For internal auditing, maintaining accurate customer and financial records, and enforcing our Terms of Service.
  • Security: To investigate potential security breaches and prevent fraudulent activity.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data and information we collect is stored securely within NexaCore's databases using appropriate security measures (including access controls). Our primary data storage is within the United States. We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, manage your account, or comply with our legal and financial record-keeping requirements.

5. Data Sharing and Confidentiality

NexaCore does not sell, rent, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties, advertisers, or outside entities for marketing purposes. Data is only shared under the following limited circumstances:

  • With our secure payment processors (Stripe/PayPal) as necessary to complete your transactions.
  • When legally compelled, such as in response to a court order, subpoena, or other valid legal process.
  • With your explicit, written consent.

6. External Services and Tracking

We do not use third-party tracking tools or cookies for user behavioral analysis. The Service may contain links or connections to third-party platforms (like Discord or Roblox). This Privacy Policy does not cover the privacy practices of those external services. We encourage you to review their policies directly.

7. Your Privacy Rights and Contact

You have the right to request access to and correction or deletion of the personal data we hold about you, subject to any legal obligations we have to retain certain records.

To exercise these rights or if you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information below:
