Appeal a Ban or Action

If you believe you were banned or received an action in error, use this page to appeal and reconnect with the NexaCore community.

Appeal Form

If you're appealing a Discord punishment, please read our rules first.

Discord Rules

  • Treat all members of the server with mutual respect - no matter what differences of opinions you may have.
  • This is a drama-free server - getting involved with or instigating drama is prohibited.
  • General swearing is allowed however, swearing at other is prohibited.
  • No matter what a person's age, religion, race, sexual identity, or disability is, discriminatory language will not be tolerated.
  • Keep all content SFW (Safe For Work) - any NSFW content will be removed and the member will be banned. This includes any gore or adult images/videos.
  • Advertising is prohibited. Trying to bypass our moderation bot to advertise will lead to an immediate server ban.
  • Abusing the ticket system will lead to a system ban - support will not be given to those who abuse the system.
  • Do not attempt to misuse any channels - this includes in text and voice channels as well as sending commands or photos somewhere other than their dedicated channel.
  • Theft, Scam, Identity Theft, Doxxing, etc is strictly prohibited - Attempting to break any laws or steal another user's personal information will result in a blacklist.
  • Any forms of political or drama causing discussion are NOT allowed - As a server, we comfort and support everyone's ideas, even if they don't align with ours.
  • Online Dating & Sexual Conduct - As a server, we strictly prohibit online dating (e-dating) and sexually suggestive conduct within its platforms, in alignment with Discord’s policies. Violators will face permanent bans.
  • Impersonation - Pretending to be another user, staff member, or organization with the intent to deceive is a serious offense and will result in immediate account suspension.
  • Copyright Usage - Using copyrighted content without appropriate permission is strictly prohibited. All intellectual property violations are forwarded to executive team for further action.
  • Fraudulent Activity - Any form of fraud—including payment manipulation, identity theft, or exploiting promotions via fake accounts Will result in immediate blacklist and be reported to legal services.
  • Follow Discord's Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.
